Football Club FullStackPlay

Your new favorite Hobby Football Club in Vienna

Welcome to the world of FC FullStackPlay

We are a motivated team that's all about having fun and enjoying the beautiful game. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just looking to get some exercise, we welcome players of all ages and genders to join us. We've got different age classes to suit everyone, from youngbloods to old timers, so don't worry if you're not the most skilled player - we'll help you improve your skills and make new friends. Our goal is to create a relaxed and supportive atmosphere where everyone can come together and enjoy playing football. So grab your boots and get ready to kick off with us!
