Your new favorite Hobby Football Club in Vienna

Welcome to the world of FC FullStackPlay

We are a motivated team that's all about having fun and enjoying the beautiful game. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just looking to get some exercise, we welcome players of all ages and genders to join us. We've got different age classes to suit everyone, from youngbloods to old timers, so don't worry if you're not the most skilled player - we'll help you improve your skills and make new friends. Our goal is to create a relaxed and supportive atmosphere where everyone can come together and enjoy playing football. So grab your boots and get ready to kick off with us!

Hear from our Members

Hannah Müller - Founder

"I founded the Club together with my husband, because from our house we would always see some boys and girls play on this neglected football field, and we thought, why not make a club for people in the surrounding area. Ever since then we achieved to become a Club that is open for everyone to join."

Roman Weinstabl - Trainer

"I joined the Club shortly after its founding. My 10 year old son is also playing in one of our junior teams. Being able to also help my players is another level of fulfillment that I'm looking towards every week."

Sertan Günes - Captain 1st Team

"A few years ago, a friend of mine informed me about this Club. After some training sessions I decided to join. I became the best version of myself playing for this club, and I hope I can play for this club for many years to come!"

Melanie Hasner - Captain Women Team

"My football career is relatively short, but my trainer and team members keep pushing me to improve with our regular training session, with me as a player, as well as me taking a teacher roal in our summercamp for the little ones. I was able to adopt many skills that don't just include playing football."

As typical for Austrian football clubs we have to pay a fee to the Austrian Football Association. With this, court costs and other occurring costs the membership fee for our club is €100,- a year. If you should not be able to cover this fee, please get in contact with our financial department so we can talk about a reduced fee and the club will cover the remaining amount. We at FC FullStackPlay want as many people as possible to play the beautiful game of football and don't want the financial aspect to be the thing that gets in the way of it.
